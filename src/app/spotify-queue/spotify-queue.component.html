During the pandemic, I became a bit restless--so I decided to start making something that I thought could improve our social gatherings (once we're allowed to have them again :)). This Spotify public queue system will let you create a "queue" for any of your public playlists on Spotify--anyone else can then follow the queue and request songs to be added onto the playlist. Only you have the power to approve and reject potential songs for your playlist.
